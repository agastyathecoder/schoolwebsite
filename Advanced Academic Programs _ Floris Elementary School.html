Advanced Academic Programs

Visit the Floris AAP Site!

Mrs. Amy Albanese, the Advanced Academics Resource Teacher (AART), will provide information about levels of service and answer questions about the screening process for part-time school-based and full-time Level IV services.

If you would like more information about AAP services, you can contact Mrs. Albanese at abalbanese@fcps.edu  or visit Advanced Academic Programs in FCPS.

Read the AAP September Newsletter!

PARENT INFORMATION MEETING

A parent information meeting will be held virtually on September 30 from 6:30-7:30PM. The AART will provide information about levels of service and examples at Floris as well as explain and answer questions about the screening processes for part-time school-based and full-time Level IV services. The meeting is open to all K-6 parents. If you have any questions please contact Amy Albanese, Advanced Academics Resource Teacher at Floris ES – abalbanese@fcps.edu

Important AAP Dates for the 2020-2021 School Year

  • Fall Screening Referral Deadline for Level IV (available only to students grades 3-6 who are new to the county) October 8, 2020
  • AAP General Information Session September 30, 2020 (6:30-7:30pm)
  • Level IV Referrals and optional additional materials (Grades 2- 6) due to AART December 1, 2020 (FIRM DEADLINE)
  • Level III (Part Time AAP) school-based screening – grades 2-5 May 2021
